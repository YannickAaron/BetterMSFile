OneDrive & SharePoint are a mess.

Files scattered across OneDrive, SharePoint sites, and Teams channels. Sync conflicts when you forget to close a file. Searching through endless team subfolders just to find that one document. BetterMSFile brings it all together.

Built for how you actually work

Every feature designed to save you time navigating Microsoft 365.

Always Online

Files stream directly from the cloud. No syncing, no conflicts, no waiting for downloads. Your data is always live and up to date.

Native Finder Feeling

SwiftUI from the ground up. Sidebar, file list, detail panel — feels like a system app.

Open & Edit Directly

Click any file to launch Word, Excel, or PowerPoint directly. No local copies needed.

Favorites & Suggestions

Pin frequently used files. Smart suggestions surface what you reach for most.

Scoped Search

Search globally or narrow results to a specific team, site, or your OneDrive. Find the right file without wading through hundreds of results.

Create & Manage Folders

Build folder structures in-app. Right-click or press ⌘⇧N to create. Delete what you no longer need.

Keyboard-First

Every action has a shortcut. Navigate, search, and manage without touching the mouse.

Drag & Drop Reorder

Arrange files your way. Drag to reorder, drag into folders to move. Custom sort order persists per folder across sessions.
