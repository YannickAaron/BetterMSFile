OneDrive & SharePoint are a mess.

Files scattered across OneDrive, SharePoint sites, and Teams channels. Sync conflicts when you forget to close a file. Searching through endless team subfolders just to find that one document. BetterMSFile brings it all together.

Always Online

Files stream directly from the cloud. No syncing, no conflicts, no waiting for downloads. Your data is always live and up to date.

Native Finder Feeling

Built with SwiftUI from the ground up. Sidebar, file list, detail panel — it looks and feels like browsing your Mac, not a web app.

Open & Edit Directly

Click any file to open it directly in its online editor. Word, Excel, PowerPoint — no local copies, no version confusion.

Favorites & Smart Suggestions

Pin your most-used files for instant access. Frequently Used learns your patterns and surfaces the files you reach for most.

Scoped Search

Search globally or narrow results to a specific team or site. Find the right file without wading through hundreds of results.

Drag & Drop Reorder

Arrange files in the order that makes sense to you. Custom sort order persists per folder across sessions.
